Bio

I am honored to be an incoming PhD student at UIUC ECE, where I will be working under the esteemed guidance of Prof. [Bin Hu] and Prof. [Geir Dullerud]. My academic journey has been enriched by serving as an Undergraduate Research Assistant under the mentorship of Prof. [Huazheng Wang]. I primarily focus on research related to Learning Theory, including Reinforcement Learning Theory, Bandits, Learning for Control, and Algorithmic Game Theory. Currently, I am dedicated to utilizing learning tools to design stable and safe control algorithms for complex nonlinear feedback systems or even vision-based control systems.
